About The Ministry

Pulling Down StrongHolds is a Christian rap ministry dedicated to spreading the Gospel through the power of hip hop music. Founded on the scripture 2 Corinthians 10:4, "The weapons we fight with are not the weapons of the world. On the contrary, they have divine power to demolish strongholds."

Our mission is to reach the lost, encourage believers, and tear down spiritual strongholds through Christ-centered lyrics and infectious beats. Combining elements of trap, hip hop, and rap with biblical truth, we create music that resonates with both young and old.

Each track is prayerfully crafted to minister to listeners where they are, addressing real-life struggles with biblical solutions and the hope found in Jesus Christ.
